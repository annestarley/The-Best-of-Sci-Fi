My History

I grew up with the Star Wars original trilogy. I probably watched all three of the movies multiple times before I had even strung together my first sentence. Star wars was always a part of my life and the series launched my love of the science fiction genre.

However, the first real science fiction movie I remember watching from start to finish was... Armaggedon! That's right, Bruce Willis saving the world! And it terrified me. I had nightmares - wherein my Dad would die - almost nightly for a week straight. I cried myself to sleep the first two nights following the viewing.

Nevertheless, none of this deterred me from my love of sci-fi. And to tell the truth, I have quite a soft spot for Armageddon still.
