History

A quick peak at the history of science fiction.
Buckle your seatbelts.

Ancient Sci-fi

Science fiction has ancient origins. Some of the original sci-fi tinted stories include Epic of Gilgamesh (Sumerian, 2150-2000 BCE), Metamorphoses (Ovid, 8 CE), and Ramayana (Hindu, 5th/4th century BCE) - which had flying cars and space ships that could go under water! ..pretty much.

(Thanks wikipedia for the facts!)

Sci-fi Novelization

In the 18th century, science fiction novels began to take shape. Among the most important are Frankenstein by Mary Shelly and The Last Man also by Mary Shelly, Go Mary Shelly!

(Another Wikipedia shoutout.)
